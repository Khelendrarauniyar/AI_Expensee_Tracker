How to Use This Web App

Follow these steps to get started with the Expense Tracker:

  1. Sign Up: Create a new account by providing your username, email, and password.
  2. Log In: Log in to your account using your credentials.
  3. Add Categories: Navigate to the "Add Category" page to create expense categories (e.g., Food, Transportation, Entertainment).
  4. Add Expenses: Go to the "Add Expense" page to record your daily expenses. Select the appropriate category, enter the amount, date, and any notes.
  5. View Analytics: Access the "View Analytics" page to see detailed reports and graphs of your spending habits.
  6. Edit Profile: Update your profile information and upload a profile picture on the "Profile" page.

We hope you find this application useful for managing your finances. If you encounter any issues or have suggestions for improvement, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.
